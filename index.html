About Us

Still Pending Games is an indie game studio that was originally formed with the goal of getting members from Bradley University's graduating class of 2020 together to finish their capstone project, Circuit Breaker. Spread out nationwide we worked together to do so and in 2021 we self published the game on Steam. Looking forward to now, we are still working together, exploring all of the wild and wacky game ideas that we have always wanted to build, bringing in new people as we go along. We're currently working on our second more fully fledged title!

We look forward to what the future holds. We are learning everything we can to build the exciting worlds, interactive experiences, and complex narratives that we want to make as developers and bring to you all as players. Follow us here and on our other platforms to see what the future holds!
